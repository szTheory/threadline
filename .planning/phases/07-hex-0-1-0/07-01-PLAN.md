### Task 2: HEX-02 — Changelog `## [0.1.0] - YYYY-MM-DD` and `### Added`

**What to do**

1. Replace the stub `## [0.1.0]` block in `CHANGELOG.md` with:
   - A line `## [0.1.0] - YYYY-MM-DD` where `YYYY-MM-DD` is the **actual** release date (ISO 8601 date only).
   - Keep `## [Unreleased]` at the top with **no** empty `### Added` / `### Fixed` subsections under it (D-01).
   - Under 0.1.0, include `### Added` with **at least three** factual bullets naming real shipped surface: e.g. `Threadline` core API, `Threadline.Plug`, `Threadline.Semantics` types, `Threadline.Capture` schemas, `Mix.Tasks.Threadline.Install`, `Mix.Tasks.Threadline.Gen.Triggers`, PostgreSQL trigger capture story — drawn from `mix.exs` `groups_for_modules` and README, not marketing copy (D-03).

**Read first**

- `CHANGELOG.md`
- `mix.exs` (`groups_for_modules`, `description`)
- `README.md` (first-screen feature list)
- `.planning/phases/07-hex-0-1-0/07-CONTEXT.md` (D-01–D-04)

**Acceptance criteria**

- `grep -E '^## \[0\.1\.0\] - [0-9]{4}-[0-9]{2}-[0-9]{2}$' CHANGELOG.md` exits 0.
- `[ "$(grep -A40 '^## \[0.1.0\] -' CHANGELOG.md | grep -c '^- ')" -ge 3 ]` exits 0 (at least three `- ` bullets in the 0.1.0 section window).
- `grep -xF '## [0.1.0]' CHANGELOG.md` exits **1** (stub heading without date must not exist as whole line).

**Done condition:** All three checks pass.
